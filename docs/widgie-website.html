How it works
Four seconds from thought
to widget
01

Summon

Press ⌘⇧W from anywhere. A command bar appears over your desktop, ready for input.

02

Describe

Type what you want in plain English. "Minimal clock with Tokyo time." "Stock tracker for AAPL." That's it.

03

Generate

AI parses your request, picks components, arranges the layout, and produces a validated configuration. ~2 seconds.

04

Materialize

Your widget fades onto the desktop. Drag it anywhere. Resize it. Right-click to edit. It persists across reboots.
